Notes for hosting:
- This is a fully static site (HTML/CSS/JS only, no backend/server needed).
- Works great on GitHub Pages, Netlify, or any static host, same as your other
  classroom sites.

  with zero setup. A student who opens dev tools and reads portal-9f3k.html's <script>
